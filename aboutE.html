E.C also provides custom event décor and design, catering, entertainment and marketing for private Individuals, institutions, corporate and Charity Organizations. Our experience and key relationships in the industry enable us to source and invite clients to your events solely based on our recommendations and the strong relationships we have fostered. Our proven success is based on our ability to partner with key suppliers on large projects, bringing together the most talented people to create an unforgettable experience.
